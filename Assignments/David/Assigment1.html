Good example

An example of a good design is the ATM, insert the credit card system.

A lot of people, when using the credit card for the first time, don't know which side of the card must be inserted. With this good design, this problem is quickly solved.

Bad example

An example of a bad design is places with multiple switches to turn the lights on/off.

If we are in an unfamiliar environment and it is the first time we are using the switches, we need to switch almost all of them to realize what is the correct one.

A simple solution to this bad design is to spread the switches by the different parts of the house/establishment or write their proper lamp in each one.
